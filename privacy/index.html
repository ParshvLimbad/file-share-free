Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 29, 2026

Thank you for choosing Drop ("we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy notice, or our practices with regards to your personal information, please contact us at electroo69og@gmail.com.

1. Information We Collect

Files and Media

Our application requires access to your device's file system and media to facilitate file sharing. We do not store your files on our servers. The application utilizes peer-to-peer (P2P) WebRTC technology to transfer files directly between devices securely. We do not have access to the contents of the files you share.

Automatically Collected Information

Our application does not require you to create an account or provide direct personal identifiable information (like name or email). However, some information is automatically collected through integrated third-party services:

  • Device Information: Information such as your device hardware model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Usage Data: Diagnostic data, bug reports, crash logs, and app interaction data.
  • Advertising Identifiers: Identifiers required by our advertising partners to serve relevant ads.

2. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you, provide core functionality, or serve advertisements. These providers have their own Privacy Policies:

  • Google AdMob: We use AdMob to display advertisements within the app. AdMob may collect and use data (such as advertising IDs and IP addresses) to show personalized or non-personalized ads.
  • RevenueCat: We use RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases and subscriptions. RevenueCat processes transaction information and anonymous user IDs to validate purchases.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To facilitate P2P file transfers and optimize app performance.
  • To serve advertisements through our ad partners.
  • To process and manage in-app subscriptions and purchases.
  • To analyze usage trends, fix bugs, and improve the overall user experience.

4. Data Security

Your file transfers are conducted over established WebRTC connections directly between devices. While we take standard precautions to ensure structural security in our app, remember that no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Children's Privacy

Our application is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under 13.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
